Fork

A fork is a powerful tactic where one piece attacks two or more enemy pieces at the same time. Knights are especially good at creating forks due to their unique movement.

Key Concepts

  • Multi-threat: One piece targets multiple pieces simultaneously.
  • Pressure: The opponent often cannot save all threatened pieces.
  • Common Forkers: Knights, pawns, and queens.

Example Position

A knight on e5 jumps to c6, attacking both the king and rook — a classic fork!

Forks are especially effective when the opponent’s pieces are close together. Train your tactical eye to spot these opportunities!
